# Governance

## Maintainer Model

The AI Regression Database is
maintained by the Korext team with
community contributions welcome.

## Lead Maintainer

Tom Bruno, Founder, Korext
Contact: maintainers@korext.com

## Decision Process

Pattern submissions are reviewed
by maintainers for:

  Reproducibility (minimum 3 out
  of 10 attempts)
  Accuracy of detection rules
  Appropriate vendor notification
  Clear correct alternative

Submissions that do not meet these
criteria are returned with feedback.

## Vendor Participation

AI tool vendors can register for
the vendor portal to respond to
patterns, acknowledge issues, and
announce fixes. See VENDOR_GUIDELINES.md.

## Community Reviewers

Community members can apply to
become reviewers by emailing
maintainers@korext.com with:

  GitHub profile
  Relevant experience
  Areas of interest

Reviewers help assess reproduction
rates and detection rule accuracy.

## Dispute Resolution

If a pattern is disputed by a
contributor, affected party, or
vendor, maintainers investigate
and make a final determination.
Disputes are documented in the
pattern's change log.

## Changes to This Document

Proposed changes to governance
require a 30 day comment period
via GitHub Discussions before
adoption.
